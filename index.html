I am an undergraduate student at the University of Washington in Seattle obtaining a double degree in computer engineering and bioengineering. I am a member of the UbiComp Lab, where I am advised by Dr. Shwetak Patel and Edward Wang. My research explores the application of computing tools to improve the quality and accessibility of healthcare. In my current work I am building and evaluating a smartphone application to detect osteoporosis using natural frequency analysis to measure changes in tibial bone density.

Through research, courses, and independent projects, I have built core skills in signal processing, machine learning, embedded systems, and fabrication. In fall of 2020 I will be applying to computer science PhD programs.
